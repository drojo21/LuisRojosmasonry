What We Do

Our Services

From outdoor living spaces to ground-up construction, we bring the same level of precision and care to every project.

▦

Backyard Pavers

Transform your outdoor space with custom paver patios, walkways, pool decks, and fire pit areas. We work with travertine, flagstone, interlocking concrete, and natural stone to create the backyard you've been imagining.

▬

Concrete Driveways

Durable concrete driveways engineered for Arizona's climate. Choose from standard, stamped, colored, or exposed aggregate finishes. We handle everything from demolition of old surfaces to final curing and sealing.

■

Stucco

Professional stucco application and repair for homes and commercial buildings. New construction stucco, re-stucco over damaged surfaces, and color matching for seamless patch repairs.

⊞

Home Additions

Expand your living space with room additions, garage conversions, and structural extensions. We manage permits, structural engineering, and construction under our KB-2 license.

☖

Semi-Custom Homes

Ground-up residential construction tailored to your vision. We build semi-custom homes that blend your design preferences with proven construction methods, all under Arizona ROC #337881.

About Us

Meet Luis Rojo Masonry LLC

Based in Tucson, Arizona, Luis Rojo Masonry LLC is a licensed masonry contractor serving homeowners and businesses across the greater Tucson metro area. Owner Luis Rojo brings hands-on expertise to every project — from backyard paver installations to full semi-custom home construction.

We hold a KB-2 Dual Residential and Small Commercial license, meaning we're qualified for both residential and commercial work. Every job is done to code, on time, and built to last.
